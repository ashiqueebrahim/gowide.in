How CafeRunner KOT Works

A simple and efficient process to streamline your kitchen operations.

Step 1: Order Placement

Orders are placed by customers and instantly sent to the kitchen through the KOT system.

Step 2: Kitchen Preparation

The kitchen staff receives real-time updates and starts preparing the order immediately.

Step 3: Order Completion

Once the order is ready, the system notifies the front desk for seamless delivery to the customer.

Why Implement a KOT System?

Reduces Manual Errors

Automating order-taking minimizes human error and eliminates misinterpretation of handwritten orders, ensuring accurate communication to the kitchen.

Tracks Inventory and Food Wastage

Real-time inventory monitoring helps prevent stockouts, and sales data analysis helps reduce food wastage and optimize inventory management.

Ensures Faster Service

Instant order transmission to the kitchen speeds up service, enhancing customer satisfaction and allowing for faster table turnover.

Simplifies Billing and Coordination

KOT systems integrate with POS systems, streamlining billing and ensuring that orders are prepared in the correct sequence, improving coordination between staff.
